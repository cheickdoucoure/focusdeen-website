Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2026  ·  NoPlan Technologies

FocusDeen ("FocusDeen," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the FocusDeen mobile application, website, and related services.

FocusDeen is a faith-based digital wellness app designed to help users reduce phone distractions, build consistency with prayer, and create healthier technology habits.

By using FocusDeen, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

a. Account and App Information

We may collect or create basic account-related information, such as:

  • Anonymous user ID or account ID
  • App preferences and onboarding completion status
  • Prayer settings and notification preferences
  • Subscription status
  • App version, device information, crash logs and diagnostic data

FocusDeen may allow users to use the app without creating a traditional username and password account. However, the app may still create an anonymous user ID to save your preferences and app progress.

b. Prayer and Progress Information

To provide the app experience, we may store information related to your prayer and focus activity, such as:

  • Prayer completion status and Deen Score
  • Streaks and daily progress
  • Focus rule settings and reminder preferences
  • Quran or daily reading completion, if used

c. Location Information

FocusDeen may request access to your location to calculate accurate prayer times based on your area. We use your location only to support app features such as prayer time calculation and related reminders. We do not sell your location data or use it for advertising. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

d. Screen Time, Family Controls, and App Selection

FocusDeen uses Apple's Screen Time and Family Controls APIs with your permission. These features allow you to select apps or categories you want to temporarily shield during Salah Focus windows. FocusDeen does not use this information for advertising and does not sell it. Apple controls the permission process — you can change or revoke these permissions through your device settings.

e. Notifications

If you enable notifications, we may collect and store a push notification token to send reminders and app-related messages. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

f. Purchase and Subscription Information

Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store your full payment card information. We may use services such as RevenueCat to manage subscription status and entitlement access inside the app.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the FocusDeen app
  • Calculate prayer times and save your preferences
  • Enable Salah Focus and app shielding features
  • Track prayer completion, Deen Score, streaks, and progress
  • Send reminders and notifications
  • Manage subscriptions and premium access
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and troubleshoot issues
  • Protect the security and integrity of the app
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

FocusDeen may use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Apple App Store and StoreKit for purchases and subscriptions
  • RevenueCat for subscription management
  • Firebase for authentication, database storage, analytics, and crash reporting
  • Expo or related services for app delivery and notifications

These services have their own privacy practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information only in limited situations:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or security of FocusDeen or our users
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition

We do not sell your personal information or share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

5. Data Retention

We keep information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Some data may be deleted or anonymized when it is no longer needed.

6. Account and Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your FocusDeen account and associated data by contacting us at the email below with the subject line: "Delete My Account". After verification, we will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable time, unless we are required to retain certain information for legal or security purposes.

7. Your Privacy Choices

You can control many privacy settings directly from your device, including location access, notification permissions, Screen Time and Family Controls permissions, and app tracking settings. You may also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

FocusDeen is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Users

If you access FocusDeen from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using FocusDeen, you understand that your information may be transferred and processed outside your country of residence.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may notify you through the app, website, or other reasonable means. The updated Privacy Policy will be effective when posted, unless otherwise stated.
